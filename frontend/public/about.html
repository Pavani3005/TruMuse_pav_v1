About Us

Our Mission

"Protecting the soul of art in the age of AI"

TruMuse exists to protect artistic identity in a time where AI-generated ministry is rising fast. Our mission is to give creators a voice, ensure ethical use of their styles, and build a fair bridge between artists and businesses.

How It all started

"Born from a need for fairness"

The idea of TruMuse was born when we saw original artworks being replicated by AI without credit. When creativity is treated like a dataset, something valuable is lost: human intention. We decided to act not by rejecting AI, but by making sure it's used ethically and responsibly. In the AI era, originality is undervalued and misused. TruMuse restores trust, traceability, and respect for creators.

We don't just detect style theft we empower creators to stand tall, be seen, and stay protected.

How TruMuse works

For Artists:

  • Detect AI generated art that resembles your style.
  • Get visibility with attribution reports.
  • License your work to interested clients.
  • Build a digital identity that's protected.

For Businesses:

  • Verify art before using it commercially.
  • Connect with original creators if similarity is detected.
  • Build ethical credibility and brand trust.

We envision a future where AI and human creativity coexist, but with clear boundaries of respect, ownership, and transparency. TruMuse is more than a detection tool; it's a stand for creativity.
