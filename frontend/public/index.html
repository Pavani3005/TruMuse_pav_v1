About TruMuse

TruMuse helps artists defend their original works from AI mimicry through ethical detection and licensing.

Why TruMuse?

In a world where AI can replicate artistic styles in seconds, original artists are often left uncredited, uncompensated.

TruMuse is built to change that.

We use ethical AI detection to identify unauthorized style mimicry, generate transparent attribution reports, and connect businesses with real creators, ensuring originality is respected and rewarded.

Who can use TruMuse?

Artists

  • Protect your art from AI mimicry
  • Get visible and credited ethically
  • License your style with consent

Businesses

  • Verify art origins before using it
  • Build brand trust through authenticity
  • Reach out to artists for a collaboration
