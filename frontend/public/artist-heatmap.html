How Heatmap Generation Works

1

Upload Original Artwork

Select your original artwork file (JPG, PNG, or other image formats). This serves as the reference for comparison and analysis.

2

Upload AI-Generated Art

Upload the AI-generated artwork that you want to compare against your original piece for similarity analysis.

3

AI-Powered Analysis

Our advanced AI system analyzes both images using computer vision algorithms to identify visual similarities, patterns, and potential copying.

4

Generate Heatmap

The system creates a visual heatmap highlighting areas of high similarity between the original and AI-generated artwork, showing potential copying regions.

5

Review Results

Examine the generated heatmap to understand the extent of similarity and identify specific areas that may require attention or legal action.
