How It Works

1

Upload Your Artwork

Select your original artwork file (JPG, PNG, or other image formats). This will be used to create your unique digital fingerprint.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI system analyzes your artwork using CLIP embeddings to understand your unique artistic style and characteristics.

3

Database Search

The system searches through our extensive database of artists to find similar styles and potential influences using vector similarity.

4

Generate Report

Receive a detailed attribution report showing similarity scores, potential influences, and recommendations for protecting your style.

5

Take Action

Use the generated heatmaps and reports to understand your artistic influence and take appropriate action if needed.
